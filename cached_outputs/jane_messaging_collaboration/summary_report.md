## Jane

### Messaging Capabilities

- **Secure Messaging (Practitioner to Patient)**: 1:1 secure messaging between practitioners and patients. Patients can reply but cannot initiate conversations. Messages are HIPAA, PIPEDA, and GDPR compliant with 128-bit encryption in transit and 256-bit at rest.
- **File Attachments in Messages**: Both practitioners and patients can attach files to messages. Supported formats: JPEG, PDF, DOCX, MP4. File size limit: 50MB. Files can be saved to patient files from messages.
- **Client Portal Messaging**: Patients can view and reply to messages through the My Account secure patient portal, accessible via web or mobile app.
- **Email Notifications for Messages**: Patients receive email notifications when they receive a new message. Practitioners can optionally enable email notifications for new messages when logged out. In-app notifications show unread message counts.
- **Messaging to Contact Profiles**: Practitioners can send secure messages to Contact profiles (e.g., family members, caregivers) using the same workflow as patient messaging.
- **Message Deletion**: Practitioners and patients can delete messages they sent within 5 minutes of sending. Deleted messages are permanent and show as deleted to both parties.
- **Message History and Privacy**: Messages are private between the practitioner and patient - other staff cannot view them. Account Owners can request message exports from Jane support team. Message threads are archived when patient profiles are merged or when practitioners become inactive.
- **Telehealth In-Call Chat**: In-session chat available during video calls for 1:1 and group telehealth appointments (up to 12 participants for couples/family therapy).
- **Telehealth Screen Share**: Screen sharing capability during telehealth video calls for collaboration and information sharing.
- **Communications Log**: Track all emails and SMS messages sent from Jane with delivery status (sent, delivered, opened, clicked, bounced, etc.). Available in staff and patient profiles for users with appropriate access levels.
